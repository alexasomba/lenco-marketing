## Types of Business Accounts

Choosing the right business account is crucial for your company's financial health. Let's break down the options available in Nigeria.

## Sole Proprietor Accounts

Perfect for freelancers and individual business owners.

**Benefits:**
- Easy to open
- Lower documentation requirements
- Full control over funds

**Requirements:**
- Valid ID (NIN, Passport, or Voter's Card)
- Utility bill or proof of address
- Business name registration (optional but recommended)

## Limited Liability Company Accounts

For registered companies with separate legal identity.

**Benefits:**
- Credibility with clients
- Limited personal liability
- Easier access to business loans

**Requirements:**
- CAC registration documents
- Memorandum and Articles of Association
- Board resolution
- Directors' IDs

## Partnership Accounts

For businesses owned by two or more individuals.

**Benefits:**
- Shared responsibility
- Combined resources
- Flexible profit sharing

**Requirements:**
- Partnership deed
- Partners' IDs
- Registration documents
