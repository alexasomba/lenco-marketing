## Welcome to Lenco

Opening a business bank account shouldn't be complicated. With Lenco, you can get your account up and running in just minutes — no paperwork, no queues, no stress.

## Why Choose Lenco?

### Free Account Opening

Unlike traditional banks, opening a Lenco account is completely free. No hidden charges, no minimum balance requirements.

### Multiple Sub-Accounts

Create up to 10 sub-accounts for different purposes:

- **Operations Account** - For day-to-day expenses
- **Payroll Account** - For salary payments
- **Savings Account** - For building reserves
- **Tax Account** - For setting aside tax obligations

### Instant Transfers

Send money to any Nigerian bank account instantly. No more waiting for business hours or dealing with transfer delays.

## How to Get Started

1. **Download the Lenco App** - Available on iOS and Android
2. **Sign Up** - Enter your business details
3. **Verify Your Identity** - Quick KYC process
4. **Start Banking** - Your account is ready!

## Features You'll Love

### Bill Payments

Pay for utilities, airtime, data, and more directly from your dashboard. Schedule recurring payments so you never miss a due date.

### Expense Tracking

Categorize your transactions automatically. Get insights into where your money goes and make smarter financial decisions.

### Team Access

Add team members with different permission levels. Control who can view, approve, or make payments.
