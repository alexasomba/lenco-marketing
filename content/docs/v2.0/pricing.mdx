# Pricing

This document records fees for transfers, collections, virtual accounts and bill payments. Examples:

- Transfer out fee — ₦43.50
- Collections (transfer in) — 0.5% capped at ₦150.00
- Bill payments commission — airtime 2.5%, mobile data 0.8%, cable TV 0.8%, electricity 0.8%
