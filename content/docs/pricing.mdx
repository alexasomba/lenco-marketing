# Pricing

## Fees

### Transfers

| Service | Fee |
|---------|-----|
| Transfer In | Free* |
| Transfer Out | ₦43.50 |

<Callout type="info">
*₦50 Statutory stamp duty charge applies for transactions above ₦10,000.00
</Callout>

### Virtual Accounts

| Service | Fee |
|---------|-----|
| Create Virtual Account | Free |
| Transfer In (Collections) | 0.5% capped at ₦150.00 |

| Cable TV | 0.8% |
